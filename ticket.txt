The task is to create a vehicle price calculator whose requirements are to reduce
amounts from the RRP based on certain criteria
Each of the following criteria will reduce a % from the RRP based on the original RRP
MOT
Over 1 year - reduce 25% price
Between 6 months and 1 year - reduce 5% price
Under 6 months - reduce 0% price
Service
If greater than 3 years - reduce 30% price
Between 3 and 1 year - reduce 10% price
Between 6 months and 1 year - reduce  5% price
Under 6 months - reduce  0% price
DamageCheck
Red - reduce 90% price
Orange - reduce 50% price
Green - reduce 0% price
